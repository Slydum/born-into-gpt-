A LIFE BEGINS INSIDE A FAMILY

Born Into

Discover the family you were born into, then watch everyone move, work, care, grow, decorate, and change across generations. Ordinary movement happens automatically—you decide when to intervene.

Life event settings

Autonomy: Your character follows school, work, meals, hobbies, errands, and sleep automatically.

Guide them: Choose a place and the character will walk there. Stop at any time, or take direct control.

Family: Siblings can be children, teens, or adults who already moved away. Households assign real beds, rooms, chores, meals, and caregivers.

Relationships: Persistent classmates, friends, crushes, phone messages, invitations, college, work, move-outs, health, and major life events continue across generations.
